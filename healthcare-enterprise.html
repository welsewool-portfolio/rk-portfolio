Objectives

Complex ecosystem of Healthcare platforms providing care experiences. Care delivery platforms support Members/Patients, Internal and External Administrators and Provider/Coaches across a few Products.
The vision for the future of Heathcare Platform is to provide a care delivery experience through a unified care platform using a single suite of tools for our users.

The goal and the result expected

  • Create a picture of how the disparate platforms unify, and show what the experience might look like for a clinician or administrator
  • Assist technology leadership in selecting the chassis for the Provider and Admin platforms
  • Assist in collecting feedback from key stakeholders and end-users
  • Help business and technology leaders plan the roadmap for the implementation of this unified platform and sunsetting of legacy applications

Platform
Mapping

It was an important step to figure out the nature of all existing platforms, their relationship with each other, and how main roles interact with them. That's why the Care Platform service mapping has been executed. On this diagram main roles, their main journey steps and main functionality they use were reflected.

In future steps it helped team and stakeholders to split business orientation for every part of the Platform.

Deliverables:
  • Clinical Platform service map
  • Care Platform service map
