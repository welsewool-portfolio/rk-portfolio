Medium

From time to time, I publish articles on Medium to share my perspective on design, AI, and the future of UX. Writing helps me reflect on my practice and contribute insights back to the design community.

Design in the age of ai

I wrote this article to share how AI is already reshaping Figma workflows. I explain how it helps with design analysis, accessibility, documentation, developer handoff, and even content — turning busywork into automation and giving designers more focus on solving real problems.

This isn't about replacing designers —
it's about augmenting them.

Preserve human interaction

I wrote this article after experiencing an AI job interview that replaced real human interaction with a bot. It made me reflect on how easily we're automating away empathy, trust, and genuine connection in spaces where they matter most. My call is simple: let's use AI to assist, not to replace, the deeply human moments that shape our lives and work.
