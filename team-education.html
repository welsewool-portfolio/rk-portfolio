TEAM EDUCATION

I conducted numerous knowledge sharing sessions and practical workshops for team members within the Organization and Projects.

Comprehensive workshop about Design Systems nature, creation process and specific aspects.

THE GOAL

The primary objective was to share the knowledge based on practical experience and deep research of the topic from real products.

Secondary goal was to give practical experience and specific exercises to the team allowing them to apply recommendations in practice and test them on real tasks.

Another goal was to demonstrate how to utilize AI in the Design System creation and maintaining process.
