WHAT I DO

I am Designer who helps companies to execute best digital (and sometimes other) solutions for products through deep research, proper problem framing, matching business and end user needs through interactive prototypes and applications design.

I conduct workshops, map processes, build information architecture,
set up design systems and processes, execute user interfaces, guidelines and design frameworks.

I turn all design processes into AI-driven frameworks to get maximum of Design.

My superpower

My SuperPower is to use systematic approach of consecutive UX activities which lead for the tangible result with minimum efforts.

I use only vital activities to get maximum result from wast possible ux exercises.

Additionally, I have front-end background from my past which makes my communication with dev team more efficient and comfortable for team members.

What else I do

Think & Deliver Framework

As a key member of many projects, I've been assigned for different stages with diverse activities required.

I provide clients with design services to find and to frame the right problem, using popular methodologies like Design Thinking.
I help teams to work in a Lean environment, so we evolve products during the production process. I also work in Agile so the scope could be done according to a specific roadmap and plan.

Design
Thinking
Workshops

In my job, I regularly meet strategic design requests and for this purpose, I practice Design Thinking methodology.

I conducted Design Thinking workshops for external clients and for the company I am currently working at. I used these workshops to solve real problems and for educational and practicing purposes.

Furthermore, I made a Design Thinking workshop for big teachers gathering coming from 24 regions of Ukraine.

Service
Blueprint
Workshops

I conducted Service Blueprint workshops for both clients and team members. Some of them were a part of a real project, some of them were for educational purposes.

I always start with mapping service on a whiteboard and then match items to complex Blueprints in digital form, so all the evidence are readable and transferable.

Journey
Mapping

Providing clients with Service Design I conduct workshops on which I and other team members mapped user journey so we can understand the real user experience.

Discovery
Frameworks

I always create a custom design discovery framework for a new product, depending on its goals and context. This allows me to stay focused on achieving the desired objectives while tailoring my approach to the specific needs of the project.

By doing so, I ensure that I make the most of my time and resources, while creating a product that truly meets the needs of its users.

Sync Workshops &
Retros

I conduct a wide range of remote and onsite design workshops to facilitate collaboration, ideation, and problem-solving with stakeholders and cross-functional teams. Depending on the project requirements, I employ various workshop formats, including design sprints, brainstorming sessions, design thinking workshops, user research workshops, team sync and retros.

I also regularly conduct retrospective sessions with both development and design teams to reflect on our processes, identify areas for improvement, and continually enhance the quality of our work.

Accessibility

I prioritize accessibility as an essential service in my work routine. Whether it is required or not, I work closely with cross-functional teams, including front-end developers, QA testers, and accessibility testing specialists, to apply best practices and follow industry standards to ensure a high-quality product.

To ensure consistency and quality in product execution, I follow a comprehensive approach, involving thorough rituals and synchronizations. I am often responsible for setting ground rules, creating checklists, and making strategic decisions that help the team apply accessibility best practices to mockups and design flows.
